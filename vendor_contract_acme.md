# Master Services Agreement — Acme Corp & CloudVantage Analytics Ltd.

**Effective date:** 1 March 2026

## 1. Services
CloudVantage Analytics Ltd. ("Vendor") shall provide customer-analytics and marketing-
segmentation services to Acme Corp ("Client"), processing Client's end-user data to
generate insights dashboards.

## 2. Data
2.1 Client shall transfer end-user records to the Vendor, including names, email
addresses, purchase history, IP addresses, and device identifiers.

2.2 The Vendor may retain all transferred data **indefinitely** to improve its models and
for any future analytics products it may develop. <!-- PLANT: storage_limitation + data_minimisation + purpose -->

2.3 The Vendor may store and process data in its data centres located in the United
States and Singapore. <!-- PLANT: international_transfer_safeguards — no adequacy / SCCs -->

## 3. Vendor Obligations
3.1 The Vendor agrees to use commercially reasonable efforts to keep the Services
available.

## 4. Incidents

**reasonable timeframe**. <!-- PLANT: breach_notification — vague, no undue-delay standard -->

5.2 Upon termination, the Vendor may retain anonymised and aggregated data.
